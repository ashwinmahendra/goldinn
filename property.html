Frequently Asked Questions

How does hotel ownership work?

Through HotelShares, you purchase shares in a property-specific LLC that owns the hotel. As a shareholder, you're entitled to a proportional share of the income generated by the property and any appreciation in value upon sale.

When do I receive distributions?

Distributions are typically made quarterly, with payments processed within 15 days after the end of each quarter. You can opt for direct deposit or reinvestment of your distributions.

What happens at the end of the investment term?

At the end of the 5-year term, the property is expected to be sold or refinanced. Upon sale, investors receive their proportional share of the proceeds, including any appreciation in the property's value.

Can I sell my shares before the end of the term?

After a 1-year holding period, you may sell your shares on our secondary marketplace, subject to availability of buyers and applicable transfer fees.
